This represents the finalized operational architecture for **MRX Module-1: The Central Orchestrator**. This workflow ensures that before a single byte of data is processed, the research intent is bulletproof, the ecosystem is exhaustively mapped, and the technical pipelines are optimized for high-signal efficiency.

---

### **Step 1: Intake Terminal (Research Intent Structuring)**

The entry point of the MRX system is a dynamic, consultative interface. The goal here is to move beyond a "brief" and into a deep understanding of the business anxiety.

* **The Interface:** A split-screen chat between the **MRX Agent (Teal)** and the **Client (White)**.  
* **The Action:** The Agent probes for the "Why." Instead of accepting "Understand EVs in Bangalore," the agent asks: *"Is this an infrastructure anxiety or a brand trust crisis?"*  
* **Outcome:** A finalized **Research Intent Statement** that serves as the North Star for the entire study.

---

### **Step 2: Ecosystem Mapping (Research Decomposition)**

Once the intent is locked, the Orchestrator performs a **Horizon Scan**. It deconstructs the problem into a 360-degree view of the market's "physics."

* **The Map:** A visual web showing the Industry Context, Primary Categories, and all potential "Neighboring Categories" (Substitutes).  
* **Source Discovery:** The system identifies all possible data "territories" where insights may hide—from niche tech forums to government registration databases (e.g., Vahan).

---

### **Step 3: Category Graph (Parameter Taxonomy)**

The system structures the ecosystem into a rigid, hierarchical graph. This taxonomy defines the exact parameters that the subsequent data ingestion must verify.

* **Example (Electric 2W):**  
  * **Electric 2W**  
    * **Premium Scooters:** Ola S1, Ather 450, TVS iQube.  
    * **Economy/Commuter:** Ola S1 X, Hero Vida.  
    * **ICE Scooters (Substitutes):** Honda Activa, Suzuki Access.  
    * **Mobility Alternatives:** Namma Metro, Rapido/Uber Moto, Bicycles.  
* **The Purpose:** This provides the "Search Logic" for the scrapers, ensuring every node in the graph is accounted for.

---

### **Step 4: Source Identification (The Exhaustive Link-Farm)**

Based on the Category Graph, the Orchestrator generates an exhaustive list of **Source Seeds**.

* **The Scope:** No limits are applied here. The system crawls for every URL, specific YouTube video, Reddit thread, and News article that mentions the parameters identified in Step 3\.  
* **Output:** A raw "Lake of Links" that covers the breadth of the internet regarding the topic.

---

### **Step 5: Source Audit (The Dipstick Analysis)**

To prevent ingestion of "noise," the Orchestrator performs a lightweight **"Scout & Peek"** operation on every URL.

* **The Metrics:** It analyzes the first few layers of content to rank the source on:  
  * **Neutrality:** Is this a sponsored review or a peer-to-peer discussion?  
  * **Relevance:** How many nodes from the Category Graph are mentioned?  
  * **Engagement Density:** The ratio of replies-to-comments and upvote-to-view ratios.  
* **Outcome:** An indexed list of "High-Signal Containers" ready for deep extraction.

---

### **Step 6: Data Estimation (Metric Quantification)**

Before triggering the heavy ingestion pipelines, the system provides a **Scale Estimate**.

* **The Audit:** The system calculates the total volume of extractable data points:  
  * **YouTube:** $X$ Comments \+ $Y$ Nested Replies.  
  * **Reddit:** $Z$ Threads \+ $A$ Comment Trees.  
  * **Reviews:** $B$ Verified Purchase blocks.  
* **The Purpose:** This allows the user to see the "Depth of the Lake" before committing resources.

---

### **Step 7: The Stop Rule (Signal Saturation Detection)**

To maintain efficiency and speed, MRX utilizes **Marginal Signal Utility (MSU)** logic.

* **The Logic:** The system ingests data in batches (e.g., 200 comments). If a new batch provides less than **5% new unique signals** compared to the previous batches, the system declares **Saturation**.  
* **Outcome:** The ingestion stops automatically once the "Physics" of the category is understood, preventing redundant processing.

---

### **Step 8: Risk Detection Module (Blind Spot Identification)**

This is the final reliability check. The system analyzes the ingested pool to detect **Research Blind Spots**.

* **The Detection:** It asks: *"What is missing?"*  
  * **Example:** "We have 90% English data; missing local Kannada-language forum signals."  
  * **Example:** "We have high Consumer data; missing B2B/Delivery Partner perspectives."  
* **The Fix:** The Orchestrator flags these risks and suggests new sources to fill the gaps, ensuring the final output is 100% reliable.
