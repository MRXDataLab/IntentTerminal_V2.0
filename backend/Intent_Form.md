# Research Intent Form

**North Star:** The research aims to strategically inform the successful launch and competitive pricing of the Ola Roadster into the premium performance motorcycle segment in Tier-1 and metro cities across India. It seeks to assess Ola's brand power against visible rivals like Ultraviolette F77, TVS X, KTM, and Yamaha, particularly by understanding if Gen Z prioritizes 'vibe' and futuristic tech, the impact of negative S1 scooter service sentiment (tracked over the last 2 years) on Roadster consideration, and the influence of Ola's 'disruptor' image versus competitors' 'legacy' trust. A key focus will be to determine the genuine intent of traditional ICE performance motorcycle owners (KTM Duke, Yamaha MT-15) to convert to the Roadster, addressing the critical concern that existing service reputation or brand perception could be a deal-breaker in this high-stakes market entry.
