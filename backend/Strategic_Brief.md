# Outtlyr Strategic Research Brief

**Research Intent:** To precisely diagnose the underlying drivers of Kellogg's Corn Portfolio's sustained YoY decline in India, specifically uncovering why robust distribution fails to translate into repeat purchases, the competitive dynamics of local price-point challengers, and the specific taste/functional proposition disconnects for current and lapsed consumers.

---

## Strategic Research Brief: Kellogg's Corn Portfolio - India Decline Reversal

#### 1. Market Context & Trigger (Business Background)
- **The Situation:** Kellogg's Corn Portfolio in India is experiencing a consistent Year-over-Year decline. This decline is characterized by a fundamental failure to convert initial trial into sustained repeat purchases, particularly within Modern Trade and E-commerce channels, despite robust distribution. The persistent Year-over-Year decline necessitates urgent strategic interventions to diagnose underlying drivers, reverse the trend, and drive sustained category growth for Kellogg's Cornflakes in India.

#### 2. The Strategic Goal (Business Objective)
- **The Objective:** This research aims to precisely diagnose the underlying drivers of Kellogg's Corn Portfolio's sustained Year-over-Year decline in India by uncovering actionable insights into consumer expectations, taste profiles, and functional proposition understanding. The objective is to inform effective strategic interventions that can re-engage lapsed consumers or attract new ones, thereby reversing the declining trend and driving sustained category growth. This decision requires understanding consumer migration towards Muesli and Granola, the impact of local competitors like Mohan Cornflakes, and why recent innovations such as Multigrain Cornflakes have failed to resonate.

#### 3. Hypotheses to Stress-Test (Research Objectives)
- **Hypothesis 1:** Despite robust retail presence, an unaddressed chasm exists between Kellogg's Corn Portfolio's product availability and consumer preference, value perception, or proposition resonance, leading to consumer migration to alternatives. (Strategic Force: **Choice Architecture**)
- **Hypothesis 2:** The failure of recent innovations, specifically Multigrain Cornflakes, to resonate with the target audience indicates a disconnect between perceived product benefits and evolving consumer taste profiles or functional requirements, contributing to sustained decline and migration towards alternative breakfast formats like Muesli and Granola. (Strategic Force: **Reinforcement Stability**)
- **Hypothesis 3:** Local competitors, exemplified by Mohan Cornflakes, are effectively leveraging lower price points to disrupt the value perception of Kellogg's Corn Portfolio, thereby eroding market share and impeding repeat purchases even in channels with strong distribution. (Strategic Force: **Value Elasticity & Competitive Energy**)

#### 4. Target Lens (Target Market / Audience)
- **The Cohort:** The system will prioritize digital footprints and conversations of current and lapsed Cornflakes consumers, as well as potential new consumers, specifically focusing on geo-located data from regions like Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Himachal Pradesh.

#### 5. The Search Perimeter (Scope: Geography, Timeline, Budget)
- **Temporal Window:** Aligned with the observed consistent Year-over-Year decline.
- **Geography:** India, with a specific focus on regions including Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Himachal Pradesh.
- **Product Scope:** Kellogg's Corn Portfolio, specifically Cornflakes and Multigrain Cornflakes.

#### 6. The Threat Matrix (Competitive Context)
- **Visible Rivals:** Mohan Cornflakes (local competitors offering lower price points).
- **Invisible Threats:** The discovery engine is authorized to scan e-commerce platforms and digital conversations for emerging or established alternative breakfast formats, specifically Muesli and Granola, which operate as "ghost rivals" influencing consumer migration patterns.

#### 7. Internal Ground Truth (Existing Knowledge)
- **Data Integration:** No proprietary client data has been provided at this stage. The research will proceed by exclusively synthesizing insights from publicly available internet data. Should internal data become available, it can be integrated as an overlay onto the internet data for enhanced validation and depth.

#### 8. Action Confidence & Stop-Rules
- **MSU (Marginal Signal Utility):** The AI will automatically halt data extraction when insights stabilize — meaning pulling additional data yields less than a 2% change in final sentiment.
- **Confidence Threshold:** Go/No-Go recommendations trigger only at 85% convergence of signals.

#### 9. Execution & Alignment
- The data generated will be used jointly by the Marketing and Product teams for strategic positioning and operational adjustments.
