#### 1. Market Context & Trigger (Business Background)
- **The Situation:** Kellogg's Corn Portfolio in India is experiencing a consistent Year-over-Year decline. This trend is catalyzed by significant consumer shifts towards alternative breakfast formats, specifically Muesli and Granola, which are perceived as more modern and healthier options. Further exacerbating the situation is the impact of local low-priced competitors. Recent product innovations, such as Multigrain Cornflakes, have underperformed, failing to reverse the decline despite achieving satisfactory trial rates. This indicates a paradox where consumers try the product but do not repeat purchases, signifying underlying issues with product resonance or value. The urgency for this research is to identify actionable strategies to reverse the declining trend and restore category relevance and growth within the Indian market.

#### 2. The Strategic Goal (Business Objective)
- **The Objective:** This research aims to identify actionable strategies to reverse the consistent Year-over-Year decline of Kellogg's Corn Portfolio in India and restore its category relevance and growth. The objective is to unlock strategic decisions regarding product innovation, brand positioning, pricing strategy, and competitive counter-measures that can address consumer migration to alternative formats, competitive pricing pressures, and the underperformance of recent innovations, ultimately leading to improved repeat purchase rates and sustained market growth.
