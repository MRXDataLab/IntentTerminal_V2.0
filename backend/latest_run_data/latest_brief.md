#### 1. Market Context & Trigger (Business Background)
- **The Situation:** Kellogg's, a masterbrand in India with established distribution, is experiencing a declining Corn Portfolio YoY. This erosion is evidenced by a consistent decline in offtakes and the failure of the Multigrain Cornflakes launch to gain traction despite substantial media investment. Previous tactical interventions, including adjustments to packaging, sizes, and product claims, have proven ineffective in reversing this negative trend.

#### 2. The Strategic Goal (Business Objective)
- **The Objective:** The primary objective is to definitively ascertain the underlying causes for the observed paradox wherein high product distribution and initial consumer trials do not translate into sustained repeat purchases for Kellogg's India Corn Portfolio. This understanding is critical for defending the core portfolio against increasing commoditization and adapting to evolving breakfast consumption preferences. Key decomposition nodes for this objective include evaluating Category Relevance amidst a Muesli/Granola shift, assessing the impact of Value Squeeze from local rivals like Mohan, diagnosing the Innovation Gap associated with Multigrain resonance, and resolving the Trial vs. Retention Paradox.

#### 3. Hypotheses to Stress-Test (Research Objectives)
- Our AI will stress-test the following specific theories using internet data:
    1. The Multigrain Cornflakes' health claims are failing to resonate with the target audience, indicating a miscalibration in **Value Elasticity** that hinders trial conversion into habitual routines.
    2. The taste profile of the Multigrain Cornflakes, and potentially core products, establishes a significant **Choice Architecture** friction point, impeding repeat purchases despite initial trial satisfaction.
    3. The observed retention collapse is attributable to a weakening of **Demand Gravity** for Kellogg's Corn Portfolio, driven by the emergence of 'Substitute Workarounds' and 'Ritual Language' shifts towards alternatives such as Muesli/Granola and local value players.
    4. The disconnect between initial product trial satisfaction and long-term habitual usage points to a failure in **Reinforcement Stability**, where initial positive experiences are not adequately fortified to establish enduring consumption patterns.

#### 4. Target Lens (Target Market / Audience)
- **The Cohort:** The system will prioritize the digital footprints and conversations of Modern Trade and E-commerce shoppers across India, with specific emphasis and granular analysis directed towards the East region.

#### 5. The Search Perimeter (Scope: Geography, Timeline, Budget)
- **Temporal Window:** A 12-month trend period, analyzing consumer shifts from the present backward.
- **Geography:** India-wide, focusing on Modern Trade and E-commerce channels, with a specific regional concern in the East.
- **Product Scope:** The entire Kellogg's Corn Portfolio, including all product sizes and the Multigrain Cornflakes variant.

#### 6. The Threat Matrix (Competitive Context)
- **Visible Rivals:** Mohan Cornflakes.
- **Invisible Threats:** The discovery engine is authorized to scan e-commerce platforms and broader digital conversations for 'Substitute Workarounds' and emerging 'Ritual Language' associated with products like Muesli/Granola, as well as to identify other unknown local sellers disrupting the breakfast cereal category.

#### 7. Internal Ground Truth (Existing Knowledge)
- **Data Integration:** The client has confirmed the unavailability of specific internal trial or repeat-purchase datasets for integration. Consequently, Outllyr will primarily rely on external digital signals for behavioral mapping and retention diagnostics.

#### 8. Action Confidence & Stop-Rules
- **MSU (Marginal Signal Utility):** The AI will automatically halt data extraction when insights stabilize — meaning pulling additional data yields less than a 2% change in final sentiment.
- **Confidence Threshold:** Go/No-Go recommendations trigger only at 85% convergence of signals.

#### 9. Execution & Alignment
- The data generated will be used jointly by the Marketing and Product teams for strategic positioning and operational adjustments.

    1. **The Ecosystem Map:** Interactive visual graph showing consumer anxieties, competitor features, and brand positioning.
    2. **The Strategic Force Scorecard:** Quantified health check measuring brand pull against market friction.
