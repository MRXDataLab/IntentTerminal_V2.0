To understand the drivers behind the consistent Year-over-Year decline of Kellogg's Corn Portfolio in India, specifically examining consumer shifts towards alternative breakfast formats (Muesli, Granola), the impact of local low-priced competitors, the reasons for the underperformance of recent innovations like Multigrain Cornflakes, and the paradox of satisfactory trial but poor repeat purchases. The ultimate goal is to identify actionable strategies to reverse the declining trend and restore category relevance and growth.
