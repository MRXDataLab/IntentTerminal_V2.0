The core research intent is to understand and reverse the consistent Year-over-Year decline of Kellogg's Corn Portfolio in India, specifically addressing the failure to convert robust distribution into sustained off-takes and repeat purchases within Modern Trade and E-commerce channels. This requires deciphering why 'modern consumers' (including HNI, school-going kids, health-conscious users, and those with weight loss goals) are shifting preferences towards Muesli and Granola, and how to counteract the commoditization by local players like Mohan Cornflakes. The study will identify effective strategies for repositioning, product innovation, and competitive response, especially given the prior ineffectiveness of initiatives like the Multigrain Cornflakes launch, packaging refreshes, and varied pack sizes, all aimed at achieving a reversal of the declining trend and driving repeat purchases within the Indian market by understanding the change in choice preference.
