# Outtlyr — Concept & Application Summary

## The Problem

Market research today is slow, expensive, and disconnected from reality. Brands spend $50K–$500K on surveys and focus groups that take 6–12 weeks to deliver insights that are already stale. Meanwhile, millions of consumers are expressing their real opinions, frustrations, and purchase decisions every day on Reddit, YouTube, Amazon reviews, and niche forums — in real time, for free.

No one is reading this data systematically.

## The Concept

**Outtlyr is an AI-powered market intelligence engine that reads the internet's unstructured exhaust and converts it into decision-grade strategic intelligence — in minutes, not months.**

Instead of asking consumers what they think (surveys), Outtlyr observes what they're *already saying* (digital exhaust). It maps the "physics of the market" — the invisible forces that drive consumer behavior — and delivers a live, evolving intelligence report that tells brands exactly why their market is shifting and what to do about it.

## The 5 Strategic Forces (Outtlyr's Framework)

Every market signal is mapped to one of 5 forces:

1. **Demand Gravity** — Is the brand pulling consumers organically, or relying on paid push?
2. **Choice Architecture Pressure** — How are consumers making decisions? What's confusing them?
3. **Value Elasticity Field** — Is the price justified? Where's the value perception breaking?
4. **Reinforcement Stability** — Are buyers strengthening or weakening brand loyalty post-purchase?
5. **Competitive Energy Field** — How are competitors changing the evaluation landscape?

## The Application — End-to-End Pipeline

```
Phase 1: Intake Terminal
    AI consultant crystallizes the research intent in 5 minutes
    ↓
Phase 2: Synthesis Review
    Generates Strategic Brief + Category Graph + Link Farming Manifest
    4 visualization modes: Knowledge Graph, 3D Graph, Flowchart, Methodology
    ↓
Phase 3: Discovery Terminal
    Autonomous internet scouting (Google Direct / Brave / SerpAPI)
    Recursive PAA rabbit hole algorithm
    LLM triage scoring → ranked hunt list
    ↓
Phase 4: Outtlyr Intelligence Map
    3D evolving live report
    Hypotheses confirmed/debunked in real-time
    AI-suggested emergent hypotheses
    Signal evidence with demography, sources, timestamps
    Intel Units economy for deeper investigation
    Final report generation at 100% convergence
```

## Key Differentiators

1. **Speed** — From vague business anxiety to actionable intelligence in 30 minutes, not 6 weeks
2. **Cost** — $0 in survey costs. Reads what consumers are already saying for free
3. **Transparency** — The client sees exactly what will happen before the study starts (Methodology tab). Every insight links back to real human quotes
4. **Evolving, not static** — The Intelligence Map is a live report that evolves as signals converge. Not a PDF delivered once
5. **Causal, not correlational** — The 5 Forces framework isolates *why* consumers are behaving a certain way, not just *what* they're doing
6. **Hypothesis-driven** — Tests the client's gut feelings against real data, and discovers new hypotheses the client didn't think to ask about

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, React 19, Three.js (3D), react-force-graph, Framer Motion, Tailwind
- **Backend:** FastAPI (Python), Gemini 2.5 Flash (LLM), Playwright (headless browser), Brave/SerpAPI
- **Architecture:** REST APIs, background job system, sessionStorage caching, multi-engine discovery

## Business Model

- **Intel Units** — clients buy investigation credits. Deeper analysis costs more IUs.
- **Study subscriptions** — monthly allocation of studies + IUs
- **Enterprise** — custom force taxonomies, team collaboration, API access
